Step 1: Box Construction

I began by laser cutting 10 exterior panels - 5 for both the lid and base - in 1/8" wood.

A latch holds the box shut.

The base and lid are attached with hinges.

There are 4 interior panels to divide the box into sections. Wood glue has been applied in the interior edges.

The nails and screws to mount the hardware were cut short with a rotary tool, as they protruded into the box.
